# Code of Conduct

## Rule 1 — No Copyrighted Content

Do not host, commit, or distribute copyrighted content in this repository. This includes but is not limited to ROM images, BIOS dumps, firmware files, or any other material you do not own or have explicit legal rights to distribute. If you are unsure whether something is permissible, do not include it.

## General Expectations

Contributors and collaborators are expected to:

- Be respectful and constructive in all communication
- Keep discussion focused on the project
