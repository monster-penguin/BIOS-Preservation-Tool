# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in the BIOS Preservation Tool!

## Before You Contribute

Please read the [README](README.md) before opening an issue or suggesting changes. It covers how the tool works, its legal boundaries, and the upstream dependency on [Abdess/retrobios](https://github.com/Abdess/retrobios).

## How to Contribute

All contributions and communication are welcome via **GitHub Issues**. Whether you've found a bug, have a feature request, a question, or just want to start a discussion — open an issue.

There are no strict templates. Just be clear and provide as much relevant context as you can.

## Guidelines

- Be respectful and constructive (see [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md))
- Do not attach, link to, or reference copyrighted BIOS files in any issue or discussion
